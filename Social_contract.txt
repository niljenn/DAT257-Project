SOCIAL CONTRACT


1. We have to inform the others, if we can't make it to the meeting, before it starts, and during the meeting
everyone has to contribute to the discussions. 

2. Everyone has to attend at least one meeting per week.

3. When a decision can't be made because of different opinions, then majority voting is used. If the voting 
is even then the scrum master decides. 

4. The goal is to hand in the assignments at latest on thursdays at 17:59.

5. After the 3rd time of being late, that person has to bring fika. Up to 5 min late is okay.
